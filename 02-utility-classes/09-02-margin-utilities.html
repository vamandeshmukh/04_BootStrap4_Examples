Margin Classes

Use .m{side}-{size} classes to adjust margins. Here side can be t, r, b, l (top/right/bottom/left), ot x, y (x is left-right and y is top-bottom). Size varies from 0 - 5, or can be auto. If margin is equal in all directions, you can use .m-{size} too.
